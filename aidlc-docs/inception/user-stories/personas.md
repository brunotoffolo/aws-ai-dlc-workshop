# User Personas

## Primary Personas

### Persona 1: Alex — Corporate Learner (Technical)
- **Role**: Software Developer / Data Engineer at a mid-size enterprise
- **Phase**: MVP
- **Goals**:
  - Upskill in new technologies relevant to current projects
  - Earn verifiable micro-credentials to demonstrate competency
  - Learn at own pace around work schedule
- **Pain Points**:
  - Generic training content doesn't match specific skill gaps
  - Existing courses are too basic or too advanced — no adaptive middle ground
  - Difficult to find time for lengthy structured programs
- **Characteristics**:
  - Comfortable with free-text topic input
  - Prefers hands-on exercises and coding challenges
  - Values depth over breadth
  - Self-directed learner
- **Platform Interaction**: Uses free-text input to request specific technical topics, prefers adaptive difficulty, pauses/resumes frequently between work tasks

### Persona 2: Maria — Corporate Learner (Non-Technical)
- **Role**: Marketing Manager at a large enterprise
- **Phase**: MVP
- **Goals**:
  - Learn business skills (data analytics, project management, leadership)
  - Earn credentials to support career progression
  - Understand technical concepts at a business level
- **Pain Points**:
  - Overwhelmed by overly technical content
  - Needs guided discovery — doesn't always know what to search for
  - Wants clear progress indicators and completion milestones
- **Characteristics**:
  - Prefers guided wizard for topic discovery
  - Favours multiple-choice and short-answer assessments over hands-on coding
  - Values visual aids, diagrams, and structured explanations
  - Needs encouragement and clear learning paths
- **Platform Interaction**: Uses guided wizard to browse categories, relies on pre-assessment to set appropriate difficulty, revisits completed materials for reference

## Secondary Personas

### Persona 3: Jordan — Training Administrator
- **Role**: L&D Specialist / Corporate Training Coordinator
- **Phase**: MVP
- **Goals**:
  - Assign relevant curricula to teams and individuals
  - Monitor learner progress and completion rates
  - Ensure training content meets organisational standards
- **Pain Points**:
  - Manual curriculum creation is time-consuming
  - Difficult to track training compliance across teams
  - No visibility into content quality before learners see it
- **Characteristics**:
  - Power user of admin dashboard
  - Assigns curricula to groups of learners
  - Reviews AI-generated content before publishing (content review workflow)
  - Needs reporting and progress views
- **Platform Interaction**: Uses admin dashboard to assign courses, reviews and approves AI-generated content, monitors team progress

### Persona 4: Sam — Content Reviewer
- **Role**: Subject Matter Expert / Senior Employee
- **Phase**: MVP (part of admin workflow)
- **Goals**:
  - Ensure AI-generated content is accurate and appropriate
  - Validate content aligns with AWS Responsible AI policy
  - Approve content before it reaches learners
- **Pain Points**:
  - AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies or bias
  - Review process needs to be efficient — not a bottleneck
  - Needs clear interface to approve/reject/request changes
- **Characteristics**:
  - Domain expertise in specific subject areas
  - Reviews content for accuracy, completeness, and appropriateness
  - May suggest edits or flag content for regeneration
- **Platform Interaction**: Receives review requests, examines generated lessons/quizzes, approves or rejects with feedback

## Tertiary Personas

### Persona 5: Dana — Department Manager
- **Role**: Engineering / Business Unit Manager
- **Phase**: Phase 2
- **Goals**:
  - View team training analytics and completion rates
  - Identify skill gaps across the team
  - Track ROI of training programs
- **Pain Points**:
  - No consolidated view of team learning progress
  - Cannot correlate training with performance outcomes
  - Needs high-level dashboards, not granular detail
- **Characteristics**:
  - Consumes analytics dashboards
  - Interested in aggregate metrics, not individual lesson details
  - Needs exportable reports
- **Platform Interaction**: Views manager analytics dashboard, reviews team completion reports, identifies training gaps
